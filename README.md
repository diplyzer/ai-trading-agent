# Diplyzer | AI Agent for Financial Markets

**Diplyzer** is an AI-powered market intelligence platform that helps traders, investors, analysts, and researchers turn market questions into actionable insights.

🌐 **Website:** https://diplyzer.com

## What is Diplyzer?

Diplyzer combines real-time market data, technical analysis, fundamental research, SEC filings, insider trading activity, institutional ownership, news, social sentiment, and AI-powered reasoning into a single conversational interface.

Instead of switching between multiple research tools, users can ask questions and receive data-driven analysis, charts, market context, and actionable insights within seconds.

## Key Features

* Real-time market intelligence
* AI-powered stock, crypto, forex, and commodity analysis
* 70+ technical indicators
* Smart Money Concepts (SMC) detection
* Automated chart pattern recognition
* Fundamental analysis and valuation models
* SEC filings and earnings intelligence
* Insider and congressional trading tracking
* Institutional ownership analysis
* Economic and macroeconomic research
* News aggregation and sentiment analysis
* Interactive charts and visualizations

Traditional investment research requires switching between dozens of platforms for charts, filings, news, sentiment, and market data.

Whether you're a trader, investor, analyst, researcher, or content creator, Diplyzer acts as your on-demand market research team.
